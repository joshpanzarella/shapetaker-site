# Designing the Aesthetic

When building the Shapetaker website, I wanted the digital experience to feel exactly like the physical hardware it represents. I pulled references from old oscilloscopes, mechanical pencils on graph paper, and deep matte aluminum. 

We used a fractal noise SVG overlay to simulate the tactile feel of a powder-coated panel, while keeping the UI stark and highly functional.
