# Filtering in Incantation

Incantation is built around a resonant filter bank. Rather than relying on a single state-variable filter, it splits the incoming signal into dozens of discrete frequency bands, allowing the user to precisely sculpt the harmonic structure.

This approach introduces significant DSP challenges, especially regarding phase cancellation and CPU overhead in VCV Rack. This post covers the optimization techniques I used to keep the module lightweight.
