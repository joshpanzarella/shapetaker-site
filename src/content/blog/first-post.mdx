# Building the Foundation

Designing a hardware module is always a journey. You start with a concept, but as soon as you hit the breadboard, things begin to mutate. The design for this module was no exception!

Here are some things I focused on:
- **Tone clarity** across the entire frequency range.
- **Ergonomics**, because a cramped panel is never fun to play.
- **Power efficiency**, ensuring it doesn't drag down the rest of the rack.
